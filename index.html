Jon Contreras, RD is a Licensed Registered Dietitian Nutritionist based in the Greater Boston area

I would like to be your personal resource on the very-often confusing relationship between human health and food. When it comes to nutrition, there are no stupid questions and frequently answers may not be completely black or white. Rather than lecturing, I would like to get to know your own unique background along with what food means to you. Then, by combining your own preferences and goals with the current science, I hope to help you create change that is both practical and sustainable!
